  1. BOOKS
  1. ''To Kill a Mockingbird'' by Harper Lee : A classic novel about justice and morality. Still one of the most powerful stories i've ever read.
  2. ''Oliver Twist'' by Charles Dickens: A timeless tale about a young orphan's journey. This classic tale of survival and hope never gets old
  3. ''The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy'' by Douglas Adams: A comedic science fiction series. If you haven't laughed out loud reading this, are you even human?
  1. MOVIES
  1. ''The Matrix'' (1999): A mind-blowing sci-fi that still holds up today.its a science fiction film exploring reality and free will.
  2. ''Inception'' (2010): A mind-bending action film about dreams within dreams. huh! what a concept dreams within dreams!
  3. ''3 idiots'' (2009): A heartwarming story about chasing your dreams and being true to yourself.
